# Analysis-of-E-Commerce-Sales-Performance-Olist-

# 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes Olist E-Commerce data to evaluate revenue trends, identify best-selling product categories, and understand customer payment preferences.
The analysis aims to generate business insights that support revenue growth and improve customer experience.

#❗ Problem Statement
Understanding sales performance and customer purchasing behavior is essential for improving business growth and operational efficiency.

#❓ Business Questions
Is the company revenue growing consistently?
Which product categories generate the most sales?
What payment methods are preferred by customers?

# 📊 Dataset
Source: Kaggle
Tables used:
Orders
Order Items
Products
Order Payments
Total records: 15,000 rows per table.

# 🧹 Data Preparation
Data Validation
Missing Value Handling
Duplicate Checking
Outlier Detection
Data Cleaning using SQL

# 📈 Key Insights
Revenue shows an overall upward trend from 2016–2018.
Furniture & Decoration is the best-selling product category.
Bed & Bath and Health & Beauty are among the top-performing categories.
Credit Card dominates customer transactions (73.8%).

#💡 Recommendations
Increase promotions during months with declining sales.
Prioritize inventory for top-selling categories.
Maintain and optimize credit card payment services.

# 🛠️ Tools Used
PostgreSQL
Kaggle Dataset
